Welcome to FeedHacker

Mute the noise in your LinkedIn feed — AI slop, promoted posts, engagement bait, hiring, and more.

Pin FeedHacker for one-click access

  1. Click the puzzle-piece icon at the top-right of your browser toolbar.
  2. Find FeedHacker in the list and click the pin next to it.
  3. The FeedHacker icon now stays in your toolbar — click it any time to open the Mute/Solo mixer.

Pinning is optional — you can unpin it the same way whenever you like.
