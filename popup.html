    How FeedHacker works

    Mute (M) hides that kind of post; Solo (S) shows only the soloed kinds.

    AI‑slop is scored and the model self‑tunes to your feed. Aggression sets how much of the feed to hide — the model aims for that share of the sloppiest posts (and it still applies when self‑tuning is off).

    The Feed display toggles change how hidden posts look — including whether long runs of hidden posts collapse into one row.
